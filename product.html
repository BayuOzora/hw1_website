Product Description

The Ray-Ban Aviator Classic is the original pilot sunglasses design that started it all. Developed in 1937 for US military aviators, these iconic sunglasses combine timeless style with exceptional performance. The classic teardrop shape and metal frame have remained virtually unchanged for over 80 years, making them a true icon of eyewear fashion.

Featuring crystal-clear lenses that provide 100% UV protection and scratch-resistant coating, these aviators are built to last while keeping your eyes protected in any environment.
